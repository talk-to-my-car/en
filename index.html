Give life to your car

Thanks to “Talk To My Car” and to the Xee device, you can now chat with your car. Just ask her questions and she will answer you !

Conversation

Here’s an example of a conversation you can have with your car. You can try it for yourself by clicking on the button at the top of the page.

“Hi ! I can’t find you. Where are you ?“

You

“I’m around Rue de Paradis 50, 75010 Paris, France. Here’s the itinerary to find me “

Your car

“Thanks a lot! Do I have enough gas to go to the Eiffel tower ?”

You

“I have enough gas to go to the Eiffel tower. It’s at 5.6 Km away, therefore I should have around 28.34 liters left in my tank.”

Your car

About

At first, this project was a personal challenge and a learning experience. Then I decided to make it accessible to all Xee customers so they could enjoy it and give me their feedback. Today, Talk To My Car is used by a few hundred people everyday. It received a nice coverage by the media and many automotive industry actors and leaders have contacted me to discuss about how we could work together in the near future. We have many great ideas! So if you have ideas on how to improve Talk To My Car, feel free to leave me a comment on my Twitter. Also, if you have ideas on how to use Talk To My Car for your company, feel free to contact me directly by email or on Linkedin.
